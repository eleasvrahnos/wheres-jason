How To Play

You start with 3 lives. A countdown will start before the reveal of each randomly-generated image.

You have 60 seconds to find Jaosn within the given image. Jaosn's reference image is seen in the example at left, as well as within the Where's Jaosn logo above. When you find Jaosn, click him and you will be awarded points based on how fast you found him (maximum points per round is 1000). Clicking the wrong place or running out of time will subtract 1 life. Lose all 3 lives and the game is over.

Each image with Jaosn is truly randomly-generated, meaning that Jaosn can be in an easy image, a hard image, an easy spot, a hard spot, etc. This means that difficulty will vary throughout the game, and luck will vary from round to round.

Your total points will be tracked and placed on the leaderboard after the game. Climb the ranks to reach #1!

Menu Button

The menu can be found by clicking the timer at the top of the page.

On the left are the red hearts that shows your current lives in the game. Next to those are volume sliders for the background music and the sound effects volume respectively. These sliders will be saved between each playthrough so you don't have to adjust them again.

On the right are the current number of points you have accumulated in the game so far, and a 'Back to Home' button. There is no limit to how many points you can earn. Clicking the 'Back to Home' button will take you back to the homepage, so use this to exit the game.

NOTE: Please try and limit usage of the forward/back/refresh navigation buttons and use the buttons given on the website instead. Some settings/scores may not be saved if using the navigation buttons.
